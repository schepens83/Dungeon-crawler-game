# Scene: Betoverd Bos (Bloedbad)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Same circular clearing of silver-barked trees, but the colors are drained and the atmosphere is bitterly cold. The stone archway with its elven statues is still standing but the statues are chipped and stained with dark dried blood. Abandoned broken treehouses hang from the canopy above like shattered bird nests. In the center where the shrine once stood, a fresh uncovered mass grave is visible — a dark rectangular wound in the earth. A single small squirrel sits at the edge of the clearing, the only living thing. Heavy oppressive silence fills the scene. Desaturated palette of cold grey-silver, dead moss-green, and dark earth tones with no warm light.

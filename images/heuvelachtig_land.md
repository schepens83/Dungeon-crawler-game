# Scene: Heuvelachtig Land

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Low-angle shot following the gentle curve of a meandering dirt trail through rolling green hills and patches of dense ancient forest. The trail surface is churned with a messy overlay of many footprints — boots and sandals of various sizes — left by a large group of people who recently passed through. Scattered at the trail edge lie dropped items: a broken wooden bowl, scraps of dirty cloth, and a bent spoon half-buried in mud. In the middle distance among the tall grass, the massive silhouette of an oversized rusty iron key is partially visible, half-hidden by wildflowers. Atmospheric haze softens the distant hills. Muted greens and warm ochre tones under an overcast grey sky.

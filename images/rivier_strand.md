# Scene: Rivier naar Zee (Schipbreuk)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view sitting low on a wide sandy beach at grim coastal twilight, looking out at the vast grey ocean where the massive river meets the sea. Mangrove trees grow in the brackish shallows where fresh and salt water mix. Dark rocks protrude from the bay, barely visible above the rising tide — a deadly obstacle for ships. Scattered across the wet sand, thick driftwood from a recent shipwreck: heavy mast beams, solid rudder planks, shattered hull timbers. A small lonely campfire burns on the sand nearby, its orange glow the only warmth, casting long shadows across the wreckage. The cold grey sea stretches endlessly to the horizon. Muted grey ocean, sandy brown, warm firelight accent. Lonely, contemplative atmosphere.

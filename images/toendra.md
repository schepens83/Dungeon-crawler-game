# Scene: Toendra

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view across a wide landscape of hard frost-covered rocky ground stretching to the horizon, dotted with sparse stunted shrubs and twisted leafless trees. The air is crisp and clear, the sky a cold pale grey. On the distant horizon, small silhouettes of nomadic horse-riders patrol at speed, kicking up faint dust trails. The riders seem to be scanning the terrain, searching. The vast open tundra offers no cover, nowhere to hide. Cold slate-grey and frost-blue palette with pale earth tones. Tense watchful atmosphere — the viewer feels exposed and vulnerable on the open plain.

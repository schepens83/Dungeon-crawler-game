# Scene: Bos (Gehangene)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Dense oppressive ancient forest where giant trees with thick gnarled trunks and heavy leaning branches block most of the sunlight, creating a dark dappled green gloom. A narrow winding road cuts through the undergrowth. From a massive oak tree beside the road, a length of thick heavy rope is tied around a high branch and hangs down, its end swaying in the still air — the unmistakable sign of swift frontier justice already carried out. In the foreground, another coil of exceptionally long and thick rope rests over a low branch, strong enough to restrain a great beast. The forest is utterly silent. Deep blacks, damp moss-green, and filtered grey-green light, grim and heavy atmosphere.

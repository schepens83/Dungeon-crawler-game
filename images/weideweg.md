# Scene: Weideweg

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Wide-angle first-person view down a winding dirt road cutting through vast green meadows under an oppressively large sky. Fluffy white sheep and brown cows graze the lush grasslands on both sides, but no shepherd or dog is anywhere in sight. The silence feels unnatural, the air thick and expectant as if the world is holding its breath. Dominating the composition, a long blood-red streak slashes across the bright daylight sky from horizon to horizon — a dark omen hanging over the peaceful countryside. Warm ochre and muted green palette with the crimson streak as the sole vivid accent. Late morning light casts long soft shadows across the meadow grass.

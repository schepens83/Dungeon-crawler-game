# Scene: Rivier aan de bergkant

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
The wide river flows upstream and disappears into the dark vertical mouth of a massive mountainside cliff, the water vanishing into black rock with a low rumble. The muddy banks on both sides are teeming with large prehistoric-looking crocodiles — some basking with jaws agape, others gliding through the dark water with only eyes and nostrils visible. On the muddy shore in the foreground, a massive bleached crocodile skull lies half-buried, its rows of wicked sharp teeth gleaming in the damp diffused light. The dead-end feeling is oppressive — no way forward, only dangerous reptiles and unyielding stone. Dark teal water, muddy brown, humid tropical green palette.

# Scene: Bovenkant van de Kloof

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Dizzying first-person perspective from the very edge of a sheer cliff, looking straight down hundreds of meters to a hazy canyon floor far below. A few meters below the precipice, a massive eagle nest is tucked into a rocky alcove, containing several large unhatched eggs among sticks and feathers. Far below on the canyon floor, barely visible through the atmospheric haze, the tiny speck of the ancient stone pyramid from the earlier canyon scene can be spotted. The sky is bright and clear, strong wind visually suggested by streaking clouds. At the bottom-left edge of the frame, a massive clawed hand grips the cliff rock, knuckles white with tension. Dark granite, bright sky, and hazy depth create a vertigo-inducing sense of scale.

# Scene: Eindeloze Woestijn (Vertrek)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view from atop the crest of a massive sand dune, looking out at an endless ocean of rolling golden sand stretching to every horizon. Behind, the same cave entrance is now a small black speck at the base of a distant dune. A large leather drinking bag hangs from a strap at the right edge of the frame, swaying slightly. The elevated vantage point suggests great height. Cinematic golden hour light paints the dune crests amber while their shadows run deep purple. Overwhelming scale of the desert dominates the composition. A sense of isolation and determination — a long journey ahead with no landmark in sight.

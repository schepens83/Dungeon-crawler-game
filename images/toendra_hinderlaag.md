# Scene: Westelijke Toendra (Hinderlaag)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Tense night scene in a labyrinth of tall jagged black rocks reaching high above, casting deep cold shadows under blue moonlight. Through a narrow gap between the rocks, a lone horseman rider passes on a horse whose hooves are muffled with cloth wrappings. In the dark foreground, crouched perfectly still in a crevice, the ambusher waits — only the glint of a massive heavy metal helmet and a set of sharp clearly non-human claws gripping blunt metal blades are visible in the moonlight. The claws are too large and too pointed to be human hands in gauntlets. Cold blue-silver moonlight against absolute black shadows, the tension of the moment before the strike. Frost-blue and slate-black palette.

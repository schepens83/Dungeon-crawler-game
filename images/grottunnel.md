# Scene: Grottunnel

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person perspective inside a deep narrow stone tunnel, the ceiling low and the damp walls pressing close on both sides. Ahead in the tunnel, a small group of gaunt starved-looking men huddle around a tiny dying campfire, their hollow faces and desperate eyes lit by flickering orange light. Large distorted shadows dance across the jagged wet rock walls behind them. The men look up toward the camera with wide fearful eyes, some scrambling back against the wall. The firelight barely reaches this far, the tunnel walls dark and oppressive. Deep cave blacks with warm firelight orange as the sole light source. Claustrophobic and tense atmosphere.

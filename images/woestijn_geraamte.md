# Scene: Zuid-Oostelijke Woestijn (Geraamte)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Bleak view of the desert edge where sand dunes transition into rocky dry ground with sparse distant bushes on the horizon. Half-buried in a smooth sand dune in the center of the frame, the massive skeleton of a large armored warhorse emerges — sun-bleached white bones polished smooth by sandstorms. Heavy rusted metal armor plates still cling to the ribcage and protrude from the sand, glinting dully in the harsh light. A long weathered leather cord tangles around the exposed ribs. Wind-blown sand slowly reclaims the remains. Bleached bone-white and rusty iron-brown against pale yellow sand, the feeling of a long-past battle preserved by the dry desert air.

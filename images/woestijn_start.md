# Scene: Eindeloze Woestijn (Ingang)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Standing in the dark shadow of a cave exit, looking out at a blinding sea of yellow sand. The jarring transition from black wet rock to hot bright dunes fills the frame — dark cave walls frame the left and right edges while an enormous sand dune looms just outside the mouth. The sun is a white-hot disk in a pale washed-out sky, its glare creating lens-like bloom across the sand. Heat shimmer distorts the distant horizon where dune after dune rolls endlessly. The cave opening creates a natural vignette of cool darkness around scorching brightness. Bleached yellows and bone-white dominate, with deep cave-black framing the composition.

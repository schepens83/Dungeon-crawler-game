# Scene: Smalle Kloof

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Low-angle perspective from the canyon floor looking up between towering jagged orange-red rock walls that frame a narrow strip of pale blue sky. Harsh unrelenting sunlight pours straight down, carving deep black shadows into the rock face while visible heat haze distorts the air. On a rocky ledge partway up the left wall, an ancient weathered stone pyramid stands with a large magnifying glass fixed solidly on top, its stone surface covered in old black burn marks. A giant eagle circles lazily in the bright sky high above, wings spread wide, casting a moving shadow on the canyon floor. Thorny scrub bushes cling to cracks in the rock. The palette is sun-bleached yellows and burnt orange against bone-white highlights.

# Scene: Ingang van een Grot (Donker)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Eye-level perspective from a narrow rocky trail looking directly into the gaping mouth of a jagged mountain cave. The cave entrance resembles a dark throat, its interior an absolute pitch-black void that seems to swallow the surrounding light. The rock around the opening is rough and wet, dark moss clinging to the edges. Outside the cave, the ambient light dims unnaturally as if shadows are being pulled toward the darkness within. Sparse scrub and loose stones line the trail leading up to the entrance. A heavy atmosphere of impending doom radiates from the void. Deep blacks and damp moss-green palette with dim grey daylight.

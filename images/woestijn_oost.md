# Scene: Oostelijke Woestijn

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Wide panoramic view of the heart of an endless desert, rolling yellow sand dunes stretching identically to every horizon with no landmarks or life in sight. The midday sun is a white-hot disk in a pale blue sky, its punishing light creating intense heatwaves that cause the horizon to shimmer and warp like liquid. Wind-swept sand ripples create hypnotic parallel patterns across each dune face. The oppressive emptiness and scale are the subject — no shelter, no shade, no variation, only infinite sand. Bleached yellows and bone-white palette under harsh high-contrast lighting, the sky a washed-out pale blue.

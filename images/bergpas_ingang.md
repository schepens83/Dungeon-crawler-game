# Scene: Ingang Oostelijke Bergpas (Griffioenen)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view peering cautiously through gaps in snow-laden tree branches toward the entrance to a narrow winding mountain pass covered in fresh snow. High in the cold pale sky, six large predatory griffons circle in a wide formation, their sharp wings cutting against the grey clouds as they search the ground below. The pass ahead looks exposed and dangerous — no cover, nowhere to hide from the aerial predators. The branches in the foreground frame the scene as if crouching behind them. Cold snow-white, dark granite, and storm-grey palette with icy blue sky accents. Tense, vulnerable atmosphere — the viewer feels watched from above.

# Scene: De Rivier (Onbegaanbaar)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view from the very edge of a steep rocky bank, looking across a massive turbulent river with fast white-capped currents crashing against jagged rocks mid-stream. The banks are steep, rocky, and slippery, lined with thick lush vegetation. No bridge, ferry, or crossing of any kind is visible in either direction. The water is deep, dark teal, and visibly dangerous. The far shore is a distant dark line — impossibly far. The steep bank drops away sharply just ahead, loose rocks at the edge. Turbulent dark teal water with white foam accents, muddy brown banks, grey-green vegetation. A sense of frustration and impassability.

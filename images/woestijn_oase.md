# Scene: Noordelijke Woestijn (Oase)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Serene sunset view of a small crystal-clear pond nestled between high golden sand dunes. The sky above is a deep gradient of purple and burnt orange, the heat shimmer finally gone with the lowering sun. A few vibrant green palm trees and lush vegetation surround the water, their reflections rippling on the surface. In the foreground, a massive heavy metal-clad gauntlet cups cool water from the pond — the gauntlet is enormous, with dark tough scales visible between the armored finger joints where the metal has worn thin. The peaceful relief of finding water after desert hardship fills the scene. Warm sunset oranges and deep purple sky contrast with the cool blue-green of the oasis water.

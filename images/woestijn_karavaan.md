# Scene: Zuidelijke Woestijn (Karavaan)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
Vast minimalist composition from the crest of a very high sand dune, looking down across an endless ocean of rolling golden dunes toward the far horizon. In the extreme distance, a tiny thin line of a merchant caravan inches slowly away — barely more than dark specks trailing a faint wisp of dust against the bright sand. The overwhelming infinite scale of the desert dwarfs the caravan into insignificance, emphasizing the traveler complete solitude. Clean bright sky above, golden sand below, nothing else. The image is about emptiness and isolation, the caravan just a reminder of unreachable civilization. Warm golden-yellow palette under clear bright daylight.

# Scene: Een Torenhoge Berg

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view looking up at an enormous jagged mountain peak, its summit hidden behind swirling dark storm clouds. Fresh snowfall blankets the ground in white, and heavy snowflakes swirl down from the grey sky, some landing close to the camera. The path ahead is treacherous — steep, rocky, and disappearing into the blizzard. To the right, a narrow opening in the rock face indicates the entrance to a high mountain pass, partially obscured by drifting snow. At the very bottom of the frame, the tip of a thick armored tail curls in the snow. Snow-white, dark granite, and storm-grey palette with icy blue highlights. Imposing, daunting scale of the mountain dominates the composition.

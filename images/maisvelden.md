# Scene: Maïsvelden

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view walking along a dirt road cutting through vast rolling fields of tall maize and corn stretching toward a distant mountain range under golden hour light. Medieval farmers working in the field pause and stare warily toward the camera, their faces a mix of recognition and fear. They keep their distance, some pulling children behind them. The tall maize barely reaches waist height from this elevated vantage point, emphasizing an unnatural tallness. The grain rustles in a gentle breeze, golden light painting everything amber. Rich ochre, amber, and warm brown palette. The farmers' uneasy reactions tell the story — something about the viewer unsettles them deeply.

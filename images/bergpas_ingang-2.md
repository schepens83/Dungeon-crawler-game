# Scene: Ingang Oostelijke Bergpas (Gereed)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view looking straight into the narrow mountain pass entrance with fresh snow and sparse snow-laden trees. The six griffons circle high above in the cold sky, their sharp wings cutting against the grey clouds. The pass stretches ahead — a gauntlet of rocky walls and deep snow. At the bottom-right edge of the frame, a massive armored clawed hand grips the hilt of a heavy weapon, razor-sharp metal talons glinting in the icy light. At the very bottom of the frame, the tip of a thick armored tail is visible, leaving a wide furrow in the snow behind. The atmosphere is tense, ready for battle. Snow-white and dark steel palette with icy blue highlights.

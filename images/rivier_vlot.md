# Scene: De Rivier (Vlot)

**Nano Banana 2 Prompt:**
First-person view looking down at a large heavy raft built from thick weathered driftwood beams, floating near the steep rocky bank of a massive turbulent river with white-capped currents. No people or figures anywhere in the scene. The raft is bound together with many coils of exceptionally thick sturdy rope — clearly constructed to support an immense weight. The churning dark teal water rushes past, spray hitting the rocks. The perspective looking down at the raft makes it look small despite its substantial size. The river banks are wild and empty, lined with thick vegetation. Dark teal churning water, grey-brown weathered wood, white foam accents.
